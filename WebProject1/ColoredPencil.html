Colored pencils:


To be fancy, colored pencils are a form of art medium made of narrow, cylindrical wood filled with a pigmented core. Basically, pencil meet color.
Colored Pencils are better for more detailed drawings! Some brands of colored pencils for artist are:
  1. Prismacolor Colored Pencils
  2. Derwent Coloursoft Pencils.
  3. Caran D'Ache Luminance.
  4. Faber-Castell Polychromos
